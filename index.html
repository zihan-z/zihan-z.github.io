周子寒

Chief Scientist · Manycore Tech Inc.

Hangzhou, Zhejiang, P. R. China

I am currently the Chief Scientist at Manycore Tech Inc. From 2013 to 2021, I was a research associate and then an assistant professor in the College of Information Sciences and Technology at Penn State University. I completed my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 2013, advised by Prof. Yi Ma. I received my Bachelor degree in Automation from Tsinghua University and my Master degree in ECE from UIUC.
